About Devin Craig

Devin Craig is a dual-threat quarterback from Lapel High School (IN), Class of 2026. Known for elite accuracy, the ability to make plays on the run, mobility, and strong leadership, Devin has been the driving force behind one of the most successful eras in program history. A four-year starter at quarterback, he has also been a four-year starter and impact player on the basketball court as a guard, showcasing his athleticism.

Academically, Devin is an Honors student with a 3.9 GPA and a member of the National Honor Society. He is also the face of the “Say No To Drugs” campaign in his community and is widely recognized for high character and leadership qualities both on and off the field.

Devin is a vocal leader, a competitor, and a proven winner — guiding Lapel to its first ever undefeated regular season, first Regional Championship, and multiple Sectional Championships.
